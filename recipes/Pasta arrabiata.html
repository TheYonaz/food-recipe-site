ANGRY PASTA!

Arrabiata in italian means angry ( in feminine tense ), it is called like that because its spicy. .

You can never go wrong with a Classic!.

Ingredients

  • 300g dried penne pasta
  • 2-3tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 green chilli, deseeded and chopped (to taste)
